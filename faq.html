Frequently Asked Questions

What is Study Inc.?
Study Inc. is a platform that acts as a bridge between tutees and tutors. In Study Inc., tutees and tutors can choose or change any schedule they want conveniently.
Who can join Study Inc.?
Anyone can join Study Inc. as there is no special requirement needed, but we especially cater to high school students.
What if I forgot my password?
Don't worry if you forgot your password. You can contact us via email and we will help you recover your account. Naturally, there will be a verification process to protect your account security.
Is it only for high school students?
Our courses are indeed structured for high school students, but you don't have to be a high school students to enroll in our courses.
